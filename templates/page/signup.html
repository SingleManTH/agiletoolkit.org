Why Signup?

Agile Toolkit is both a software and a collection of web services, everything to help you get your project done faster. The toolkit you download and build your application on can take advantage of AgileToolkit.org by tapping into wide-range of add-ons, offer you with code-sharing, registration, allows you promote your services and products.

Marketplace

The number one reason to be registered on AgileToolkit.org is it's growing marketplace. This is the place where free and commercial add-ons are shared and are sold. Marketplace is integrated into every Agile Toolkit installation out there, so consider publishing your code here.

Community

Here you can always find like-minded developers and be discovered by companies who are willing to hire, contract or consult ATK specialists. On-site forum, articles, cookbooks and interactive documentation will help you in exploring and learning agile toolkit.

AgileToolkit.org community will always be free and registration is entirely optional.
